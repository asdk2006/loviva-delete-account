Account Deletion Policy

Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Loviva account and associated data, you may request account deletion through the application or by contacting us.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Loviva application.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information
  • Profile photos
  • Messages and chats
  • Matches and likes
  • User preferences
  • Subscription information

Data Retention

Some information may be temporarily retained for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
