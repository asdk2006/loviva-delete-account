🗑️ Loviva Account Deletion

Users of the Loviva application may request permanent deletion of their account and associated data.

🔐 Sign in to delete your account

Don't have an account? Sign up

⚠️ Delete Account

To request account deletion:

  1. Open the Loviva application.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion request.

Alternatively, send an email to:
support@loviva.app

Please include your registered email address.

When an account is deleted, the following information is permanently removed:

  • Profile information
  • Photos
  • Messages
  • Matches
  • Preferences
  • Subscription information

Some records may be retained temporarily if required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Processing time: up to 30 days.
